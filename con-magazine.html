Texas A&M College of Nursing Magazine

Category: Web Design | UX/UI | Visit Site

Along with the redesign for the Texas A&M College of Nursing, the college created a Magazine highlighting accolades, staff, and students over the past decade.

Although the magazine lives within the College of Nursing site, I incorporated distinct design elements that mimic the print version. The navigation structure itself emulates turning pages on a magazine, while simultaneously giving the user the option to return to the College of Nursing site.
