The Sound Mind Project

Category: Web Design | UX/UI | (Work In Progress)

Mental Health is one of my biggest passions. With the Sound Mind Project, my goal is to create a search engine for mental health resources and help simplify the process of obtaining mental health services.

At the moment I'm still working on conceptualizing a lot of the components, but part of the overall structure for this project is to provide a simple and intuitive interface that makes it easy for user to find what they need.
