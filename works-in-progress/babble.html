Babble

Category: Application Design | Hologram Design | (Work In Progress)

Babble was an idea I came up with after being inspired by hologram illusions. Back in Coachella 2012, one of the live performances by Snoop Dogg featured a full fledged Tupac hologram that blew the crowd away.

After researching what made that performance possible, I came across the illussion 'Pepper's Ghost'. This illusion essentially led me to conceptualize an idea for creating holograms, but in real time. I questioned how this would be possible, but at the moment I'm conceptualizing an application that would allow users to utilize a prerendered avatar that would capture facial expressions.

By creating this three-dimensional avatar, I would then construct a device to replicate the 'Pepper's Ghost' illusion and thereby creating a new twist that mimics something you would see in Star Wars.
