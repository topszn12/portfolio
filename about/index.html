About Me

My name is Emmanuel but my friends call me Manny. I am a Web & Information Designer by title, UX/UI Designer by career standards, but above all else, a Creative through pure passion.

My admiration for creating stems from my love of imagination, conceptualizing my ideas and then bringing those ideas to life. As a creator, what I enjoy most is seeing the process unfold. From start to finish, I find myself guided by my entrepreneurial instincts and utilize my creative tools to build something special.

I believe creativity unlocks the potential to discover the endless possibilities of 'What if?', and regardless of whatever field or career opportunities come my way, I make sure to approach every single one of those opportunities by always asking "What if?".

TLDR Version: I like to learn and make stuff. Usually with code and graphics.
