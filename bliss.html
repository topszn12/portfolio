Bliss

Category: Graphic Design | Branding

In one of my graphic design courses, I was tasked with creating a bowl based restaurant. My restaurant, Bliss, was an acai bowl restaurant created to serve college students. My reasoning for going with an acai bowl restaurant was to provide a healthy, quick, and delicious alternative to the millions of fried chicken restaurants in College Station.

I enjoyed this project because it taught me a lot about branding and it allowed me to further develop my graphic design skills.
