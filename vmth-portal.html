Texas A&M Veterinary Medical Teaching Hospital Online Portal

Throughout my time as a marketing assistant at the Texas A&M Veterinary Medical Teaching Hospital, I was tasked with assisting the Information Technology team to develop an online portal for referring veterinarians. As an assistant still in school, this project was a big opportunity for me. It allowed me to showcase my development skills as well as design skills.

*Note: Due to HIPPA compliancy, I'm only able to share concept screens and not actual information. In case you were wondering, yes puppies also have privacy rights when it comes to medical care.
