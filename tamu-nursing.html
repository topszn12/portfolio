Texas A&M College of Nursing Redesign

Category: Web Design | UX/UI | Visit Site

Throughout the redesign of the College of Nursing website, I served as the primary designer for this project. This was an opportunity for me to not only serve as a lead but for me to fully showcase my design capabilities, leadership skills, and creative process.

My biggest priority while redesigning the old site, was creating a new side navigation structure that made it easier to browse related pages. One common issue amongst our users was that there was a ton of confusion involving the old structure. With the new design set in place, we received great feedback from both students and faculty that we began implementing the navigation structure on other Health Science Center based sites as well.
